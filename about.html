    • About Us

    We are a 3 man F1 in Schools Team who have, so far, placed 2nd in the UK East Anglia Regional Finals and are currently preparing to participate in the UK National Finals. We have designed and manufactured a model F1 car that cleared the 25 meter track in 1.401 seconds and overall placed 4th in the race! For the nationals, we are planning on merging our design and Aquila's, the other Development Class team from Aylsham High School, design as they managed to clear the track in 1.296 seconds. This will mean that we will have a very fast car to beat the other teams in the Nationals with.
